Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Book a Touchpoint Meeting With My Account Manager?

On the homepage of your portal, click the 'Schedule Meeting' button.
A calendar will appear, showing available times.
Select a time that suits your schedule.
Once selected, a Microsoft Teams meeting will be automatically set up.

How Do I Add Users to My Account?

To add users to your account, simply follow these steps:

  1. From the home page, navigate to the 'Accounts' section.

  2. Enter the email address of your coworker in the designated field.

  3. The system will automatically create an account for them.

  4. Upon their first login, they will be prompted to set a personal password.

  5. This process ensures that your team members have immediate access to the necessary tools and information in the portal.

How Do I Download Files From the Interstellar Server?

  1. Navigate to the 'Files' section in your portal.

  2. Click on the 'Download' link. Your server password will be displayed on this page.

  3. Enter your server password to access the file system.

  4. You will then see a list of files, which may include marketing program deliverables, screenshots, and other relevant documents.

  5. Right-click on the file you wish to download and select 'Download'.

How Do I Upload Files to the Interstellar Server?

To upload files to the Interstellar server, simply follow these steps:

      Go to the 'Files' section in your portal.

      Click on the 'Upload' button, which is separate from the download options.

      Enter the same server password you use for downloading files.

      You will be redirected to a page where you can select and upload files from your local system.

      Uploaded files will be stored in a shareable folder, which is accessible to your account manager.

      Note on File Upload Limits:

Please be aware that we have set upload limits to ensure optimal performance and security for all our clients. For any single file upload, the maximum size limit is 20GB. If you need to upload a file larger than this limit, we kindly request that you inform your account manager prior to the upload. This will allow us to make the necessary accommodations and ensure a smooth and efficient process for transferring large files.

What is My Client Score? How Do I Improve It?

Your client score, ranging from 0 to 100, reflects how closely aligned you are with our agency's processes and communications. It decreases when there are unapproved deliverables or unread messages in your account. To improve your score:

      Regularly check and respond to messages on your homepage.

      Promptly review and approve deliverables.

      Stay engaged with your account manager for timely updates and feedback.

      Improving your client score ensures smoother collaboration and maximizes the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.


Staff Admin Questions


What is the special purpose of the Client Admin Role?

The Client Admin Role is special in that there can only be one per client. It is only the Client Admin Role that can create or modify the information on the Client Intake Form.

The Client Admin Role can create, modify, and delete entities of the Client Standard Role.
